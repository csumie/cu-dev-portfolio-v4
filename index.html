ABOUT ME

I am a versatile developer and designer specializing in crafting responsive websites that blend client visual aesthetics with a strong focus on accessibility.

With over 18 years of dedicated experience in eCommerce spanning multiple commerce management systems, my expertise lies in designing seamless and intuitive web experiences tailored to the distinct requirements of small businesses and Fortune 500 companies alike.

Throughout my career, I've cultivated my digital design skills through collaborations with professional teams and fellow creatives, gaining experience in optimizing photography, managing and building content and product pages, developing wireframes, and delivering innovative solutions in fast-paced environments.

  1. Digital Design

    As a digital designer in eCommerce, I bring ideas to life by creating online visuals that make shopping experiences engaging and enjoyable. From product images to banners and promotional materials, I design, edit, and optimize visuals that stay true to client and brand identities. Working closely with studio photographers, I ensure every visual—from pre-production to post-production—improve and maintain the shopping experience.

  2. Email Marketing

    I stay focused on designing and building email campaigns that engage customers, showcase products, and drive sales by crafting visually appealing layouts, select the right imagery, and optimize emails for performance and responsiveness across all devices and platforms, ensuring a consistent and effective experience.

  3. Ecommerce

    My Ecommerce experience ranges from product management to overseeing website functionalities—maintaining product updates and improving site performance and user experience. My responsibilities are managing a CMS's business side and collaborating closely with product development teams, creative designers, marketing teams, SEO managers, and QA to ensure the website aligns with business goals. Additionally, tests are conducted across different environments to guarantee compatibility and smooth functionality across all platforms.

  4. Product Management

    Overseeing the lifecycle of products from conception to their live appearance on e-commerce sites. Managing extensive product information and SKUs, ensuring each product is accurately described, categorized, and tagged for optimal searchability. I was responsible for product building on sites, handling new products and product variations, and building integration with the inventory and commerce management systems through CSV-driven automation. This process includes rigorous quality checks to confirm all product information (pricing, availability, etc.) is displayed correctly while continually optimizing the user experience by refining product taxonomy.

  5. SEO

    When new products are introduced, I collaborate with SEO specialists, ensuring that product data and attributes are implemented correctly from the entry stage. This process continues into monitoring performance and organic traffic to assess product visibility—if traffic is affected, the SEO team provides updates that involve both content adjustments (keywords) and technical tweaks (site speed, mobile optimization). 

  6. Web Development

    As a front-end web developer working with CMS systems, I manage, create, and maintain websites by building web pages. I use native CMS tools to create pages, but I also inject custom code—like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript—to add extra functionality ensuring the site is user-friendly, looks great, and works well on any device. My focus is on creating a seamless experience for visitors.

  7. UX Design

    As a UX Designer in the eCommerce space,  I create user-centered designs that make online shopping smooth and enjoyable. A background in front-end web development helps bridge the gap between the design and technical aspects of building a website. I design and develop aesthetically pleasing layouts focusing on being responsive and adapting smoothly to different screen sizes to streamline the entire shopping experience, making sure it’s fast, accessible, and visually appealing no matter where it’s viewed.

  8. Agile Management

    My experience in agile management allows me to track all my projects—website redesigns, launching new product, or optimizing user experiences—and execute efficiently while keeping stakeholders informed. I use tools like Monday.com and GitHub to create a roadmap, prioritize tasks, and track progress in real-time. At the end of each sprint, I review what went well and what needs improvement and make adjustments before moving forward.
